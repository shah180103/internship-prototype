Astro: Revitalizing a Media Giant for the Digital Age

Client Astro Malaysia
Services Branding, Paid Ads, Social Media
Timeline 6 Months
Result 300% ROI

The Challenge

Despite being a market leader, Astro was facing a plateau in new digital subscriptions. Their legacy branding wasn't resonating with the younger, digital-native demographic (Gen Z & Millennials), who were migrating to streaming competitors.

They needed a fresh digital identity and a high-performance campaign to drive app downloads and sign-ups without alienating their existing loyal customer base.

Visualizing the new digital-first brand identity.

The Solution

We approached this with a two-pronged strategy: Modernize the Visuals and Optimize the Funnel.

  • Data-Backed Creative: We analyzed competitor ads to find the "hook." We shifted from generic TV-style ads to short, punchy, user-generated content (UGC) style videos for TikTok and Instagram Reels.
  • Precision Targeting: We utilized Facebook's lookalike audiences based on their high-LTV (Lifetime Value) existing subscribers to find similar users.
  • Frictionless Landing Page: Our web team redesigned the sign-up flow, reducing the steps from 5 to 2, significantly lowering the drop-off rate.
